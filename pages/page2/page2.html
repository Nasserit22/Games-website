Yu-Gi-Oh! Master Duel

About Game

Yu-Gi-Oh! Master Duel is a free-to-play digital collectible card game based on the Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game,
developed and published by Konami for Microsoft Windows, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, Android, and iOS.
It was released January 19, 2022 to positive reviews and rapidly climbed to the top of the Steam charts.
On February 6, Konami announced the game had been downloaded over 10 million times.
